    About us

    Our Company was established in 1975. Because of growing business and export our products by ourselves to worldwide markets, we changed our company name to” Jin Cheng Co., Ltd” in 1998.

    Jin Cheng company specializes in designing and manufacturing Mechanical press machines, Pneumatci press machines, shearing machines, and all kinds of special machines for special purposes.

    We not only provide best products to clients, but also can ODM or OEM for clients with our best repair service.

    If you are interested in our products or have any inquiries, please feel free to contact us. We will be very glad to serve you.
